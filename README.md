1. Swiggy Food Delivery Analytics Dashboard

2. 📌Project Summary : 
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze Swiggy food delivery sales, customer behavior, restaurant performance, and regional sales trends.
Designed the dashboard to help stakeholders monitor business performance and make data-driven decisions.
Created dynamic visualizations and KPI reports for sales tracking and performance analysis.

3. 🎯 Business Objectives : 
Analyze overall sales performance.
Track customer ordering behavior.
Identify top-performing restaurants.
Evaluate state-wise sales contribution.
Understand food category preferences.
Monitor daily, weekly, and monthly sales trends.

4.🔍 Key Insights : 
Generated ₹53.01M in total sales from 197K orders.
Achieved an average order value of ₹268.51.
Highest monthly sales recorded in May and August (₹6.79M).
Lowest monthly sales recorded in February (₹6.27M).
Maintained a strong customer satisfaction score of 4.34/5.
Received over 6 million customer ratings.
Vegetarian food contributed 66.59% of total revenue.
Non-Vegetarian food contributed 33.41% of total revenue.
KFC emerged as the top-performing restaurant with ₹4.2M sales.
Karnataka generated the highest state-wise sales with ₹5.5M revenue.
Weekend sales were higher compared to weekdays, with Saturday showing peak performance.

5.💡 Business Recommendations : 
Increase marketing efforts during high-performing months.
Strengthen partnerships with top-performing restaurant chains.
Expand promotional campaigns in Karnataka and other high-revenue states.
Introduce targeted offers for Non-Vegetarian food categories.
Leverage higher weekend demand through special discounts and combo offers.
